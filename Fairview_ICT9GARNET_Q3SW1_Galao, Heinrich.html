STRANGER THINGS
SYNOPSIS

The episode started on a fictional small town in Hawkins, Indiana, that is plagued by a hostile alternate dimension which is known as "Upside Down". This "Upside Down" caused Will Byers to be stuck in the alternate dimension, which caused problems for his family and friends. This peculiar incident starts a chain of events such as involving secret experiments, terrifying supernatural forces and one strange little girl.
